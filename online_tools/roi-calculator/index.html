How We Calculate ROI

This calculator estimates savings from the values you enter. The defaults are illustrative planning assumptions, not independent benchmark claims.

Manual query cost = Team size × Queries/day × Minutes/query × Hourly rate ÷ 60. This is the total labor cost of writing and debugging SQL queries manually.

With agentic analytics, the efficiency gain slider models how much manual query-writing time could be reduced. Adjust it to match your own evaluation or pilot results.

Extra capacity shows how many additional queries your team could handle with the time saved — effectively increasing your data team's throughput without adding headcount.

FAQ

How accurate are these estimates?
The calculator is directional. Actual savings vary by query complexity, data stack maturity, review requirements, and team structure. Use your own pilot data for a final business case.
What counts as a "query" in this calculator?
A query includes the full cycle: understanding the business question, identifying relevant tables, writing SQL, debugging errors, optimizing performance, and formatting results. Agentic analytics automates the middle 4 steps.
Does this include the cost of the InfiniSynapse license?
No — this calculator shows gross time savings. For a net ROI including license costs, contact our sales team for a personalized quote based on your team size.
